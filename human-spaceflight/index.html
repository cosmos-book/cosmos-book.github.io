In the book we have an infographic showing the total amount of time every astronaut has been in space (defined as above 100km). To make this we compiled a data set of astronauts. You can browse our data for each individual astronaut on Github. If you spot any mistakes, do please let us know or, even better, fix it for yourself in the repository and submit a pull request.

Only includes humans who have reached altitudes of at least 100 km. Flight durations are based on take-off/landing so short flights over-estimate the time in space. You may also be interested in our map of astronaut birthplaces, who is in space, our human spaceflight timeline, or our summary of human spaceflight.

Based on data compiled from the NASA Information Summaries Astronaut Fact Book, the NASA History Office, spacefacts.de and other sources for Cosmos: The Infographic Book of Space / Stuart Lowe & Chris North
