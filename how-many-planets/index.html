How many planets are there in the Solar System?

The answer is surprisingly varied. Choose a year.

In 1600 there were seven known planets which included the Sun, Mercury, Venus, Moon, Mars, Jupiter and Saturn. With the development of astronomical telescopes new objects were found which, although for a time were known as planets, were later re-classified as moons, asteroids, and dwarf planets.
